Here at LaunchPad, we've created a space for brilliant minds to share their latest projects, products, and startups. Reach out to those you see potential in, or garner funding and support from those who see potential in you!


Here at LaunchPad, our only goal is to uplift others. Our initiative is to build a community, or better yet a family, which fosters innovation and helps see it through. The platform's biggest inspiration was the show "Shark Tank". We wanted to bring the benefits of opportunities like Shark Tank to a wider audience, which could only be done via the help of our community. If we may bring one inventor's dream to fruition, then we would gladly consider it a success. Join our family today and see how far you can go!
